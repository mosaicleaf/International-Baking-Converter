What is the difference for each countries tab?

  • Australia uses the international metric cup.
    1 tablespoon equals 20 mL.

  • Canada uses metric and U.S. customary.

  • The U.S., Liberia, and Myanmar use U.S. customary.
    1 tablespoon is equal to about 15 mL.

  • The U.K. uses metric except if it is an older recipe. The older recipes use imperial.
    1 tablespoon is equal to 18 mL.

What do I Need section

When you go into What do I need? it will show you what to use to get as close to the decimal displayed as possible. If you don't have anything before the decimal you must put a zero in front or it will not work.
